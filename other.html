BEYOND RESEARCH

A look into the creative, personal, and community-driven side of my life.

MIT Mirchi

South Asian Fusion Dance Team · MIT

I’m a choreographer and dancer for MIT Mirchi, our South Asian fusion dance team that combines Bollywood, classical, hip-hop, and contemporary storytelling. It’s where my love for culture, music, and teamwork meet.

Creative Storytelling & Tech Reflections

YouTube Channel · Personal

On my personal YouTube channel, I share creative reflections on technology, design, and storytelling. This video explores how ideas evolve from sketches to real-world projects — the same iterative curiosity that drives my research.

Python for Kids

YouTube Series · Educational Outreach

A short educational video series I created to introduce Python fundamentals — loops, variables, and conditionals — in a fun and visual way for young learners. Teaching kids to code reminded me that clarity and creativity go hand in hand.

Writing & Reflection

Book-in-progress · Medium Essays

I’m currently writing a book that explores the intersection of artificial intelligence, empathy, and design — a meditation on how technology can help us rediscover what it means to be human.

On Medium, I publish essays connecting AI research to broader ethical and creative themes. For me, writing is a form of debugging — not of code, but of thought.

Community, Leadership & Music

Sigma Kappa · StartLabs · South Asian Association · Ukulele & Volleyball

As a member of Sigma Kappa, I’ve helped organize philanthropy events that build community across MIT. I’m also an executive member of the South Asian Association and a contributor to StartLabs, MIT’s premier entrepreneurship club, where we explore how ideas can scale into impact.

Outside of academics, you’ll find me playing volleyball, experimenting with the ukulele, or lost in a new playlist — because rhythm, in any form, keeps me grounded.
