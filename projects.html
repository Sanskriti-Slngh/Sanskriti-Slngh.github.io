PROJECTS & RESEARCH

Selected publications, research projects, and independent ventures.

PneumoXttention

IEEE ISPA 2021

A CNN ensemble for pneumonia detection in chest X-rays, designed to reduce human error. Validated against radiologists on RSNA & NIH datasets, achieving strong F1 scores.

Mentors: Dr. Colin-Whitby Strevens · Dr. Sussane Soin

Automated Coronary Calcium Scoring

IEEE MIT URTC 2022

Developed a semi-supervised U-Net model to assess heart attack risk from non-gated CT scans. Improved mean absolute error by 91% and F1 score by 32% using cropping techniques.

Mentors: Weicheng Dai · Ms. Kirti Tamhane

CheX-Nomaly

arXiv Preprint

Siamese U-Net model with contrastive learning to localize thoracic abnormalities across 14+ diseases. Focused on generalizability and reducing perceptual diagnostic errors in chest X-rays.

Mentors: Chenyu You · Ms. Swetha Bhattacharya

Mask R-CNN for Brain Tumor Segmentation

arXiv Preprint

Applied image subtraction with Mask R-CNN to segment heterogeneous brain tumors from MRI scans. Achieved DICE coefficient of 0.75, outperforming standard baselines.

Mentors: Dr. Sussane Soin · Ms. Sunita Gehlot

Minimization of False Negatives/Positives

arXiv Preprint

Introduced a method for reducing false negatives and false positives in binary classification by adjusting inputs post pre-training. Demonstrated consistent improvements across multiple datasets.

Mentor: Ms. Sujatha Raghu

Water Consumption Analysis

Independent Project

Developed unsupervised ML models and distance algorithms (K-Means, DTW) to estimate household water usage per appliance. Enables conservation and cost-saving insights through usage disaggregation.

Mentors: Ms. Swetha Bhattacharya · Weicheng Dai

News–to–ArXiv Pipeline

Independent Project, 2025–Present

Built a Python-based pipeline mapping 500+ news queries into arXiv search tasks. Uses LLMs and heuristics to retrieve top-10 relevant papers from 50k+ articles, creating an automated system for research topic discovery.
