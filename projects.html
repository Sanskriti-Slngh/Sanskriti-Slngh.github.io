PneumoXttention

IEEE ISPA 2021

A CNN ensemble for pneumonia detection in chest X-rays, designed to reduce human error. Validated against radiologists on RSNA & NIH datasets, achieving strong F1 scores.

Mentors: Dr. Colin-Whitby Strevens · Dr. Sussane Soin

Automated Coronary Calcium Scoring

IEEE MIT URTC 2022

Developed a semi-supervised U-Net model to assess heart attack risk from non-gated CT scans. Improved mean absolute error by 91% and F1 score by 32% using cropping techniques.

Mentors: Weicheng Dai · Ms. Kirti Tamhane

CheX-Nomaly

arXiv Preprint

Siamese U-Net model with contrastive learning to localize thoracic abnormalities across 14+ diseases. Focused on generalizability and reducing perceptual diagnostic errors.

Mentors: Chenyu You · Ms. Swetha Bhattacharya

Mask R-CNN for Brain Tumor Segmentation

arXiv Preprint

Applied image subtraction with Mask R-CNN to segment heterogeneous brain tumors from MRIs. Achieved DICE coefficient of 0.75, outperforming standard baselines.

Mentors: Dr. Sussane Soin · Ms. Sunita Gehlot
